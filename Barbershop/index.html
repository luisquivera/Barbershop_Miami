Our goal is to provide our customers with the best possible barber haircut and to give you the haircut you actually asked for. We strike a unique balance between old fashion barbering and modern hair cutting technique.

Haircuts

Every single one of our haircuts is finished with a straight razor shave around the ears and on the back of the neck. We do modern and retro haircuts, including: Pompadours, Flattops, Fades, Mohawks, Faux Hawks, Fro hawks, Tapers and much more!

Styling

We also offer water and oil based pomades, spray wax, gels, shampoos and several different mustache waxes. Stop in for your next haircut or just to check out the shop.
